Events and Marketing Executive

Full Time/Freelancer
Gurgaon

Key Responsibilities

  • Prepare client presentations, pitch books and brochures.
  • Work on strategy and the marketing campaigns.
  • Would be required to work on Customer Acquisition through Events and Promotional Campaigns.
  • Should be able tofollow up on leads, network to expand user base of the company.
  • Make presentations, develop, maintain and manage company portfolios.
  • Maintain assigned user base while developing new users.
  • Deliver advertising or illustration proofs to customers for acquisition.
  • Attend sales meetings, industry trade shows, and training seminars in order to gather information, promote products, expand network of contacts, and increase knowledge.

What We Require?

  • Education from a reputed institute or graduates with requisite experience.
  • 0-2 years of experience in customer acquisition andusermanagement.
  • Willing to travel (both local and domestic).
  • Good communication and inter personal skills.
  • Prior exposure to organizing seminars or marketing related activities will be a plus.
  • Should be able to confidently handle customers.
  • Inquisitive and Self Starter to meet the targets set by the marketing team.
  • Candidates who understand the working of a start-up venture and having entrepreneurial frame of mind would be preferred.
  • Candidate should have strong communication skills and pleasing personality.
